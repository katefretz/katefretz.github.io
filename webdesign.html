Here is my work from assignment 1 in Web Design I. In this assignment we had to describe four different categories for our future life goals, I chose knowledge and career, family, travel, and lifestyle. In this assignment I learned that there are two goals in mind when creating a portfolio; mastery and performance. “Mastery goals generally refer to students' desire to develop knowledge or skills in the light of self-referential standards, while performance goals center chiefly on students' concern about outperforming peers on the basis of normative standard.” (Chau 51). With this I learned that I have both goals in mind when creating a portfolio. This was the first assignment I had hands on experience with html. Most aspects of this assignment was creating paragraphs, headings, and bullet points. However, this assignment taught me how to upload images into an html document.
